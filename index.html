Namaste Solar HubSpot Form Integration

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, WordPress, Divi WordPress Theme, HubSpot, LambdaTest

The Marketing Department at Namaste Solar uses the HubSpot Marketing Automation software for Lead Generation and customer insights. They have a number of HubSpot generated forms embedded throughout their WordPress website, including their homepage. One day these forms lost their formatting and were displaying incorrectly. They were having trouble figuring out what went wrong because they had a bunch of css from multiple developers and site versions.

I was hired by the Namaste marketing department to dig in and troubleshoot the formatting issue as well as go through their existing CSS and optimize it by removing an unnecessary code.
